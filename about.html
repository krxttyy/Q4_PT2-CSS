The Meaning of Rithika

Derived from Sanskrit, Rithika is a modern girl's name commonly meaning "small river," "stream," or "passage." It represents the concept of flow and is often associated with tradition, truth, and righteousness. Much like its origins in South India, the name symbolizes a personality that is graceful, knowledgeable, and determined.
